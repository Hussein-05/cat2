About Hussein Rentals Agency (H.R.A)


At Hussein Rentals Agency, we believe everyone deserves a home they love. Whether you’re renting, buying, or exploring property opportunities, our team is here to guide you every step of the way.

Who We Are

Hussein Rentals Agency (H.R.A) is a trusted real estate company dedicated to connecting clients with affordable, comfortable, and secure housing solutions. Since our establishment in 2007, we’ve proudly helped hundreds of families and individuals find their dream homes across Kenya. With a strong commitment to transparency and customer satisfaction, we continue to bridge the gap between property owners and tenants through innovative, reliable, and customer-centered services.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To simplify the housing process by providing transparent, reliable, and customer-friendly real estate services to tenants, landlords, and property investors across Kenya.

Vision: To be Kenya’s most trusted and preferred real estate partner — where every client finds a place they can proudly call home.

Our Services

  • Property management and maintenance
  • Real estate sales and marketing
  • Tenant support and housing consultation
  • Investment advice for property owners

Why Choose Us?

At Hussein Rentals Agency, we value people as much as property. From the first inquiry to the final move-in, we ensure a smooth, personalized, and professional experience tailored to your unique needs. Our commitment to honesty, reliability, and customer care sets us apart — giving you peace of mind and confidence in every decision.

The Founder
